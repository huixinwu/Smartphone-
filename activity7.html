Two products in one row. Each product should have a title, image, brief description, price, and a link to open a modal window. All elements should have styles. 4
Two modal windows, one modal window for each product. The modal window should have a header (title and close link), main section with image of the product (product name, brief description, and price), and a footer (brief close message and a close link). All elements should have styles. 5.5
