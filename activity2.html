Activity 2: links and navigation tabs

Description

Activity 2 refers to building an app about the city of New York. You will apply your basic knowledge of aligning texts, adjusting images, and creating hyperlinks in images and texts. You will also use unordered list for hyperlinks (internally and externally) of the app

The information and images about New York City can be retrieved from the internet.
